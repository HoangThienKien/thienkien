Who we are

Rooted in Vietnam.
Refined for the world.

Thiện Kiến began as a conviction: that Vietnamese culinary heritage — its botanicals, its rituals, its depth — is not folklore to be preserved behind glass, but living knowledge to be shared with care and precision.

We work at the intersection of culture, craft, and commerce — curating experiences that open doors between Vietnam and the world, one gathering at a time.

善見 · Sudarshana

In Buddhist cosmology, Sudṛśa is the heaven of clear sight — a realm where purity meets profound understanding. In Hindu philosophy, the Sudarshana Chakra dispels ignorance and restores harmony.

These are not metaphors. They are operating principles.

What we do

Four disciplines.
One philosophy.

Every engagement with Thiện Kiến is built on the same foundation: deep knowledge of Vietnamese culture, precision in craft, and respect for the people on both sides of the table.

01

Culinary Experience Design

Private dinners, teabreaks, and ceremonial feasts — designed to carry Vietnamese culinary identity into contemporary formats. For corporate gatherings, diplomatic events, and private occasions.

02

Giftset Design

Bespoke gift sets built around Vietnamese botanical and artisanal goods. Named ingredients, honest sourcing, and packaging that reflects the value of what's inside.

03

Gourmet Offering Design

Product development and curation for the TKG botanical line — from formulation discipline through Naula, to retail-ready packaging and market positioning.

04

Cultural Experience Design

Full-scale cultural events that bring Vietnamese traditions to life for international audiences — archery, herbal sachet-making, ceremonial tastings, and curated storytelling. Past clients include diplomatic missions across Southeast Asia and the United States.

Our first product

Botanical wellness,
from the source.

TKG is Thiện Kiến's botanical wellness brand — the first commercial expression of our belief that Vietnamese herbal practice deserves a place in the world's premium wellness conversation.

Named ingredients. Honest formulation. The Naula method — a whole-plant preparation discipline rooted in Vietnamese botanical practice. Laboratory tested. No shortcuts.

TKG is not a product line. It is the beginning of a botanical house — structured to grow through careful M&A with companies that share the same value system.
