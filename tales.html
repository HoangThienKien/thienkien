Introduce

Immerse in Vietnamese Culture Through Food & Local Experiences

Have you ever wanted to truly immerse yourself in Vietnamese culture? Thiện Kiến is a cultural storyteller that offers private parties designed to engage all five senses in the rich tapestry of Vietnam.

Thiện Kiến began with a simple yet profound idea: to share the richness of Vietnamese culture through authentic culinary experiences. A space where traditions were not just observed, but lived and savored. Where every gathering — especially a private one — became an opportunity to forge lasting connections.

Chapter I · 2023

Exploring the Culinary Delights of Tết Đoan Ngọ — A Teabreak Experience at TECx2023

At TECx2023 — a showcase of groundbreaking innovations in Fintech, VR/AR, Blockchain, and Biotech — Thiện Kiến sponsored a teabreak celebrating Tết Đoan Ngọ (the Duanwu Festival), inviting attendees to savor the rich culinary heritage of Vietnam.

Where technology met tradition, a bowl of fermented glutinous rice and a cup of herbal tea became the most memorable part of the day. The first public step: Vietnamese botanical culture introduced to an audience of innovators.

TECx2023 Tết Đoan Ngọ Teabreak Corporate Event

Chapter II · 2023

Experiencing Vietnamese Culture Through All 5 Senses

Vietnamese culture is a vibrant tapestry of traditions, colors, and flavors. Thiện Kiến enhanced this cultural journey by offering encounters that stimulate all five senses — not just a tea break or fingerfood, but immersive travel experiences designed especially for private parties.

Instead of observing, guests lived it. Scent, texture, sound, sight, and taste — each element curated to carry the essence of Vietnam into the room. Allowing guests to truly embrace what it means to experience a culture through its food.

Immersive Experience Private Party Multisensory

Chapter III · 2023

The Untold Stories of "Stories of Gratitude"

In a heartwarming celebration that felt both intimate and grand, Thiện Kiến hosted Stories of Gratitude — a private gathering honoring partners and clients who played vital roles in the journey over two years.

Inspired by Thanksgiving traditions from the United States and Canada, alongside the New Rice Festival celebrated by ethnic minorities in Vietnam's Central Highlands, the evening wove together themes of appreciation, community, and cultural richness. A table where gratitude became the main course.

IV
Chapter IV · 2024

The Idea of the "Gratitude Fund" — Empowering Young Lives

In today's fast-paced world, many children face challenges that can feel insurmountable. Yet cultivating gratitude can inspire resilience and hope.

The Gratitude Fund, founded by Thiện Kiến, aims to promote gratitude among young people while celebrating Vietnamese culture and directly supporting underprivileged children. Through meaningful programs and financial backing, the fund not only addresses immediate needs but paves the way for children to chase their dreams and unlock their potential. Because culture, when shared with intention, has the power to change lives.

Gratitude Fund Social Impact Youth Vietnamese Culture
V
Chapter V · 2024

Tradition and Innovation at New Energy NEXUS Vietnam's 5th Anniversary

New Energy NEXUS Vietnam marked its 5th anniversary with an event that artfully blended cultural traditions with modern ideas. At the heart of the festivities was the Cấp Sắc Ceremony — a significant coming-of-age ritual in Dao culture.

Thiện Kiến organised an experience that honored New Energy NEXUS Vietnam's five-year journey through innovative yet traditional encounters. Innovation and heritage, seated at the same table. The arrow struck the bullseye. Applause and cheers erupted. In another corner, the aroma of local herbs drew guests together to craft their own fragrant sachets — inspired by the traditional sachet-making custom of the Dao people.

New Energy NEXUS Cấp Sắc Ceremony Dao Culture Anniversary Event
VI
Chapter VI · 2022

How Thiện Kiến's Journey Began — Vietnam Summer Fair 2022, Huế

Thiện Kiến's story is a journey woven with culture, tradition, and innovation. The pivotal moment unfolded at the Vietnam Summer Fair 2022, hosted in the beautiful city of Huế.

Co-founded by two dedicated individuals, this event was not just a celebration of Vietnamese heritage — it showcased the talents and creativity of artisans and cultural organisations from across the nation. Here, Thiện Kiến took its first steps: a beginning rooted in the belief that Vietnamese culinary culture deserved to be shared, with care and precision, with the world.

Origin Story Vietnam Summer Fair 2022 Huế Founded
